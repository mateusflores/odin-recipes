Banana smoothie

Home

Delicious and healthy!


Sweeten your life with a banana smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 orange, peeled and quartered
  • 1/3 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup water or milk

Steps

  1. Roughly chop the banana and orange quarters, and then add to a blender.
  2. Top with yogurt and water (or milk).
  3. Turn blender on and blend until creamy and smooth.
  4. Taste, and then adjust with honey if needed.
