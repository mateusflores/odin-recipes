Look at this irrisistible fried egg!


One of the easiests recipes, i'm sure that you can do it :D

Ingredients

  • One egg (avoid the rotten ones, it doesn't smells good)
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Put some oil in the pan.
  2. Carefully break an egg in the pan.
  3. Fry for 60 seconds.
  4. Remove the egg and add salt.
