This is a easy, quick and delicious meal that you can do! (but be careful, it's not heathy :v)

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cup noodles (You can choose your favorite flavor)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a separate container. Fold back lid and add boling water to inside fill line.
  2. Close lid and let stand for 3 minutes.
  3. Stir and enjoy!
